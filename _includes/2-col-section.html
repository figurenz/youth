Data empowers us

To work towards a better future for disabled people in Aotearoa, we need to build compelling, evidence-backed stories to advocate for change.

Data is a vital tool to help us understand, measure, manage, and assess issues and the impact of the changes we make.

But for a long time, disabled people have struggled to access data. Learn about the history of disability data in our guest essay.
